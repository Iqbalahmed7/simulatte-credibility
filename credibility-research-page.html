Methodology

How it's measured

Every study uses a consistent evaluation framework. Holdout questions are pre-designated before any calibration run begins — zero topic anchors provided.

Distribution Accuracy (DA)
DA = 1 − TVD = 1 − Σ|realᵢ − simᵢ| / 2

TVD is total variation distance between the real population distribution and the simulated distribution across all response options.

Human ceiling: 91% — Iyengar et al., Stanford 2023. The best human raters achieve approximately 91% DA when predicting survey distributions.

Variance protocol: 3× replications per study with the same routing logic. Reported as ±Xpp across runs.

Pipeline Architecture

WorldviewAnchor dimensions: Institutional Trust (IT), Social Conservatism / Cultural Traditionalism (SCP/CT), Collective / Individual (CS/IND), Ethno-National Security Protectionism (ESP), Religious Salience / Moral Foundationalism (RS/MF). Continuous per persona.

Option-Vocabulary Anchoring (OVA): exact survey question language and answer options embedded verbatim in persona system prompts.

Holdout protocol: 5 questions pre-designated per study. No topic anchors provided during calibration. Evaluated identically to calibration questions post-run.

Persona Generator: REST API. Domain-specific tiers (DEEP for India v2). 40–80 personas per study depending on population heterogeneity.
